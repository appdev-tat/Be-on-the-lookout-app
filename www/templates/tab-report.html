Report suspicious activity

When you report trafficking, the hotline staff will work with the FBI and local police to recover the victims. Approaching traffickers is dangerous for you and their victims and could lead to problems in the prosecution of the traffickers.

Please notify dispatch or your manager of the suspicious activity.

What to report

If possible, take photos and email them to the hotline.

For law enforcement to open an investigation on your tip, they need key pieces of information. Specific tips helpful when reporting to the hotline would include:

Cars

Are there cars involved in the suspicious activity?

Record the make, model, color, license plate, etc.

People

Who is involved in the activity?

Make note of height, weight, ethnicity, hair color, eye color, age, etc.

Date/time

When did the suspicious activity happen?

What day and what time?

Location

Where did the suspicious activity take place?

Do you have an address? Be as specific as you can.
