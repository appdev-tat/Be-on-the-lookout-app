Build a report

For law enforcement to open an investigation on your tip, they need key pieces of information. Record what you know about the incident.

Have you come into contact with someone you suspect is a victim?

Check the red flags that apply:

The victim has restricted or controlled communication— not allowed to speak for self The victim has a disheveled or unkempt appearance, is alone, scared, or crying The victim is a minor traveling without adult supervision The victim offers to exchange sex for a ride, meal, etc. The victim does not know the person who is picking him/her up The victim shows any acknowledgement that she/he has a pimp and is making a quota The victim has signs of branding or tattooing of the trafficker's name (often on the neck) The victim has a lack of knowledge of the community or his/her whereabouts The victim is not in control of his/her own identification documents (ID/passport)
Do you see any other people (buyer or traffickers, other) involved in the suspicious activity?

Describe what makes them suspicious and record their height, weight, ethnicity, hair color, eye color, age, etc.

Are there any vehicles involved in the suspicious activity?

For all vehicles involved, record how they're involved and the make, model, color, license plate, etc.
